# Results

Primary outcomes were analyzed in paired pre-post format (n=23 participants).
The knowledge total score changed from median 4.0 pre to 5.0 post (exact p=0.0166).
The comfort domain score changed from median 4.0 to 15.0 (exact p=3.447e-05).
The attitude domain score changed from median 19.0 to 22.0 (exact p=0.2308).
